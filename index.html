Thoughts on Design

Is design art? And is art design? I believe this question is what defines me as a designer - I seem not to agree with most people on this debate.

The definition of art is to evoke an emotion or get people thinking. I can't imagine a designer not hoping to achieve an emotional response or connection with their product.

Everything that is designed should evoke emotions and make people feel something. Why? Because everything we design is part of a brand, and a brand always stands for something.

So not only should things be functional, but it should evoke the right emotions as well. Therefore, I consider good design to be both a design and a piece of art. Design can be art, and art can be design.

It's all about balance. A balance of both functional and beauty, both the rational and the emotional, and be both art and design at the same time.
